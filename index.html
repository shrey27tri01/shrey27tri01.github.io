Bio

I am a Junior at IIIT Bangalore, pursuing Integrated M.Tech. (B.Tech + M.Tech) in Computer Science and Engineering.

My research interests lie in Graph Theory, Software Engineering, and Blockchain protocols. I am also passionate about and like working on projects in the areas of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

Currently, I am working as a research intern at the Software Engineering Lab at IIITB, under the supervision of Prof. Sujit Kumar Chakrabarti, where we are working on a solution for the Application Processing Problem. I am also a Tezos India Fellow, where I am building a Risk Identification and Management Platform for DeFi protocols on the Tezos blockchain.

I have also previously worked as an affiliate at the Web Science Lab at IIITB where I worked under the supervision of Prof. Srinath Srinivasa on a Capacity Based Access Control portal for the Indian Urban Data Exchange (IUDX).

I also like developing web applications and contributing to open source projects. Apart from that, I like playing and following football (ardent FC Barcelona fan!), chess, and badminton. I also like reading novels, mostly fiction and thriller (am currently reading Shogun by James Clavell!).
